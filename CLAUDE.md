## State Tracking (required in all sessions)

This applies whether you are running inside Oversight, Cursor, VS Code, or any terminal.

After every significant action, update `project_state.md` in this project root:
- Update "Last Session Summary" with what just happened (include ISO date)
- Tick off completed TODOs, add new ones discovered
- Log results and metrics under "Results"
- Append any new approach under "Approaches Tried" with outcome noted
- Update "Status" and "Blockers" if they changed

Keep entries timestamped. Do not delete history — append to lists.
Be brief but precise. Use markdown tables for metrics where appropriate.
